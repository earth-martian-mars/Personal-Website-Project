About Me

Greetings! I'm an indie game developer with a decade of experience honing my craft as a passionate hobbyist. My journey began with a fascination for storytelling and interactive experiences, which led me to explore various game development tools and languages. Over the years, I've dabbled in everything from pixel art to 3D modeling, and from coding mechanics to composing soundtracks and sound effects. I thrive on the creative freedom that indie development offers, allowing me to experiment with unique concepts and bring my imagination to life. My projects often reflect my love for engaging narratives, immersive gameplay, and distinct art styles. I believe that games have the power to connect people and evoke emotions, and I strive to create experiences that resonate with players.
